Making a Difference in Prince George

The Salvation Army has been serving the Prince George community for decades, providing hope and support to those in need. Our mission is to meet human needs without discrimination.

Through our various programs and services, we help thousands of individuals and families each year by providing:

  • Emergency Food Services - Food bank and meal programs
  • Housing Support - Emergency shelter and transitional housing
  • Community Programs - Youth programs, senior services
  • Thrift Store - Affordable clothing and household items
  • Disaster Relief - Emergency response and recovery

Every donation, every volunteer hour, and every act of kindness helps us continue our mission in Prince George and the surrounding communities.
